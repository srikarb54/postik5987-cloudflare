This Privacy Policy describes how the application postik5987 ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information obtained through TikTok services when you use the App.

1. Scope of This Policy

postik5987 is a private automation application created and operated solely by its owner for managing and publishing content to the owner’s own business (brands) TikTok accounts. The App is not offered to the public and does not provide services to external users.

2. Information We Collect

The App accesses limited information from TikTok during authentication and content publishing, including:

  • Basic profile information (such as open_id, avatar, and display name)

The information obtained from TikTok is used exclusively to:

6. Third-Party Services
